Objectives

 

The <Onto-SWEBOK> project aims at engineering a formal ontology of Software Engineering based on the outcomes of the Guide to the Software Engineering Body of Knowledge SWEBOK project, which addresses several requirements for advanced Semantic Web applications, including the following:

 

§          Traces back to SWEBOK in order to document the inclusion of concepts

§          Integrates learning object definitions connected with <Onto-SWEBOK> terms

§          Provides explicit "integration points" with other existing commonsense ontologies

§          Links to known Software Engineering frameworks like the Unified Process as a mean for validation.
